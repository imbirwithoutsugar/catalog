Print with any plastic without supports (bridges enabled).

More info in this video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OZc5GKi1V

Official site: https://docs.lilka.dev/

**Variants:**
There are variants for 11.4 mm, 14 mm, and 16 mm height (for different battery heights).

**Features:**
Use original screws directly into plastic — no additional hardware needed.
