The goal of this design is to make a максимально compact (22 mm thick), simple, and easy-to-print case that can fit a BL-5C (or BL-6C) battery, or another battery up to 53×34 mm.

To allow printing the front part face-down, the display mounting was adjusted: with 5 mm standoffs, the screen surface is level with the button rims.

**Additional materials:**
- M2×5 mm standoffs
- M2×3 mm screws
- DIP-16 or DIP-8 IC socket (optional)
- Self-tapping screws with 2.3...2.7 mm outer diameter and 13...18 mm threaded length

**Build requirements:**
- USB module soldered flush (no gap)
- microSD module mounted with board offset
- Select, Reset, Start buttons can be 4, 5, or 6 mm tall
- Display mounted on 5 mm standoffs
