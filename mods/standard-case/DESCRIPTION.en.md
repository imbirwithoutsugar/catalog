Case for the Lilka board where the display is soldered to the board and mounted on 5 mm standoffs.

This case does not provide a headphone output opening or a pin header opening for extra modules.

**Required materials:**
- 5 mm display standoffs — 3 pcs
- Heat-set M2 threaded inserts, 3 or 4 mm long — 6 pcs
- M2 screws to mount the board to the upper shell, 4–6 mm long — 2 pcs
- M2 screws to join case halves, 8–12 mm long — 4 pcs

**Printing notes:**
Printed with PET-G, 0.6 mm nozzle, 0.16 mm layer height, 0.5 mm line width. There is one area in the lower shell where supports may be useful if your printer handles bridges poorly.
